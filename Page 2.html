A Beginner's Guide to Sustainable Fashion

A fun chat with Emma Edmondson, who is a leading consultant within the sustainable fashion industry and a creative director for a successful fashion brand. A deep dive into how issues like overproduction can be addressed within sustainable fashion that play a huge role in the contribution of increased carbon emission and how this could be tackled. A key message from the discussion is “I cannot do all the good that the world needs. But the world needs all the good that I can do”, highlighting the urgent need for change.

How to Repair Your Clothes

A quick guide to the simplest ways to bring your torn clothes back to life. This short guide addresses not only the most common repairs but also some great handy tips and tricks that will be useful in daily life.

TED talk by Amit Kalra

A deep insight into what happens to the clothes that are left unbought? Highlighting the issues of what consumers think is last season coats, trousers end of being user but on the downside most of it ends up in landfills. This informative talk addresses such issues of how fashion has become a waste problem and Amit Kalra highlights the urgency to fix it. Some creative ideas can not only save the environment but also allow companies to gain a competitive edge.
